Welcome to Vasavi College of Engineering

Vasavi College of Engineering, established in 1981, is a premier institute in Hyderabad offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

About the College

Affiliated to Osmania University and approved by AICTE, Vasavi College of Engineering is committed to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and preparing students for successful careers in the engineering field.
